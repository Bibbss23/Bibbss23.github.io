Honda
CBR1000RR-R SP

We Present you with the 30th Anniversary Edition of CBR1000RR-R to celebrate decades of racing glory. Built and verified on the racing circuit, the RR series is created to focus on total control over the racetrack. This is a homage to the 1992 CBR1000RR, where the traditional "FireBlade" nickname was given to the flagship models of Honda super sports bikes. First introduced in the 1980s, Honda CBR series has since carried the pursuit of maximizing the thrill of maneuvering for those who desire higher sports performance.
